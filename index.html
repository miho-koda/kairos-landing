The Thesis
In the 1980s, a small group of funds started using computers to analyze markets. It seemed silly at the time, and quantitative trading is now obvious. We are at the same inflection point. The next Renaissance, Bridgewater, and D.E. Shaw will be built on AI.

The biggest funds in the world have been slow to adapt. We are building the fund whose entire infrastructure is built around AI from the ground up.

How It Works
1
Hundreds of markets scanned in real time for mispricings.
2
Diverse AI models independently forecast probabilities, engage in structured debate, and veto weak signals. No consensus = no position.
3
Execution happens solely when risk-adjusted math clears predefined thresholds. All reasoning is logged, auditable, and reviewable.

Human-in-the-loop governance · Self-improving agents · Full decision transparency
